About

Leandra is a qualified Counselling Psychologist who studied and lectured at the University of the Witwatersrand. She started her career in a mixed family practice and then went on to set up her own private practice in Johannesburg.

After some time overseas, she relocated to Cape Town where she has been very involved in the community sector, while working for an NGO called the Sisanda FunDaytion. Within the NGO she is responsible for the overall management and operations of the NGO as well as co-ordinating various programmes.

In addition to her community work, she has established a psychology practice, with a special interest in working with children, specifically children under 5 as well as infants.
